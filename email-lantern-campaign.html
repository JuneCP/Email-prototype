✦ The Story Behind It
The Enamel Tealight Lantern
in Aqua — Crafted for
golden evenings.
There's a particular kind of quiet that arrives just after sunset — when the day softens and you want your space to feel like a refuge. That's exactly the feeling this lantern was made for.

Inspired by Moroccan artisan lattice work, every tiny cutout is purposefully placed so that when your tealight flickers inside, it casts a constellation of golden dots across your walls and ceiling. The aqua enamel finish pairs a calming sea-glass tone with the warmth of a copper-toned interior — a balance that feels effortlessly summery and endlessly sophisticated.
